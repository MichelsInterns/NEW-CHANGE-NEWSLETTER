Returning info
Here is what you need to know to return your truck and equipment on your last day.
Returning your truck?
Contact Tony Wright, Equipment Manager, for truck pickup!
Tony will need the date your truck will be last used, the pickup address, and a point of contact at that location.
Trucks can be picked up anywhere, so don’t worry about location!
Items to provide your manager on your last day:
- Access Card to Buildings
- Keys
- ID Badge
- Credit Card/Debit Card/Fuel Card
- Computer Equipment
- Password for Computer
- Equipment/Tools/Safety Equipment
- Vehicle/Toll Passes
Questions? Contact us!
If you have any questions, please contact us or your manager!
